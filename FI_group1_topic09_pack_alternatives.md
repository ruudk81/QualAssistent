### Size-Promise Alignment
**Small Size Acceptable IF:**
1. Product is positioned as "quick fix" not long-lasting (line 944)
2. It's actually dense/concentrated (lines 935-936)
3. No claims of extended effect (line 943)

**Small Size Problematic IF:**
- Promises long-lasting effect (contradiction from earlier discussion)
- Seems too small to be effective (initial reaction line 933)
